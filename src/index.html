Purpose/Theme - The Cowboy Way

Design team chose a western theme entitled "The Cowboy Way" for the group's pariticipation in CSS Zen Garden. The theme features an earthy color platte, a western font family, leather textured background, and western-styled elements such as a cowboy hat and horseshoes. Design team will work closely and share the workload to implement this design without changing any html.
